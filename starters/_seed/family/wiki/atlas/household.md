# Household — areas map

A hand-kept map of the household's standing areas, linking the durable
notes that matter across them. This is synthesis (`atlas/`), not capture —
it points down into `library/`, `people/`, and `efforts/` rather than
restating them.

## Money

- Budget reviews and tax documents in `library/`.
- Recurring expenses and the vendors behind them (`people/`).

## Health

- Per-person records and the medical cases they belong to in
  `efforts/cases/`.

## Travel

- Upcoming and past trips under `efforts/trips/`.

> Edit this map freely — add an area, prune one. The kit seeds it as a
> starting point, not a fixed structure.
